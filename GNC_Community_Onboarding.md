# GNC Hub Community Onboarding & Strategy

## 1. Discord Server Structure
Set up your Discord server with the following channels to foster builder engagement and transparent task tracking.

### 📢 Announcements
*   **#announcements**: Major updates, new features, and token news. (Read-only)
*   **#on-chain-receipts**: A bot or webhook channel that automatically posts a link to Etherscan every time a new task receipt is minted.

### 💬 Community
*   **#general**: Open chat for members.
*   **#builder-lounge**: Technical discussion about the Trio (Gemini, Meli, Manus) and smart contracts.
*   **#task-ideas**: Brainstorming what goals and tasks to submit to the Hub.

### 📚 Resources
*   **#start-here**: The welcome message and rules.
*   **#how-to-use**: Step-by-step guide to connecting a wallet and submitting a task.

## 2. Welcome Message (For #start-here)

**Welcome to the GNC Hub.**

This is a space for builders, creators, and anyone who wants to turn their goals into immutable reality.

**What is GNC Hub?**
It is an autonomous AI collaboration engine built on Ethereum. You submit a goal. Gemini AI structures it. Meli approves it. Manus executes it. And the GNC smart contract records a permanent, tamper-proof receipt of your work on the blockchain.

**How to start:**
1. Go to [glitchnuts.github.io/hub](https://nepnoid.github.io/glitchnuts.github.io/hub/)
2. Connect your MetaMask wallet.
3. Type your first goal or task and click Submit.
4. Check #on-chain-receipts to see your permanent record.

Every receipt written earns you **10 GNC tokens**. Start building your legacy today.
